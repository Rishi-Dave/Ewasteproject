About Us

ERS is an organization that collects electronic waste through a organized process and delivers it to e-waste facilities. Through this process, we can efficiently recycle electronic waste which would go to a dump instead and pollute our atmosphere. The world has limited resources and it is important for everyone to play a part. By participating in our goal, you can also make a positive impact on our world.
